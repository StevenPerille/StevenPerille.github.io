Metric Definitions

Understanding the data is the first step toward optimizing performance. Below are definitions for the core metrics featured on this dashboard.

Statcast Terms

Exit Velocity
The speed of a baseball as it comes off the bat. It is measured in miles per hour (MPH).
Launch Angle
The vertical angle at which a ball leaves a player's bat after being struck.
Barrel
A batted ball with a combination of exit velocity and launch angle that generally leads to a minimum .500 batting average and 1.500 slugging percentage.
